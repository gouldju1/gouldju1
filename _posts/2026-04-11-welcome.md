## What to expect

I'll be writing about topics at the intersection of AI, data engineering, and engineering leadership — things I'm thinking about, building, or have learned the hard way.

Some likely themes:

- **MLOps in practice** — what actually works at scale vs. what looks good in demos
- **GenAI architecture** — RAG, agents, evaluation, and the unglamorous parts
- **Engineering leadership** — building teams, aligning on vision, shipping things that matter
- **AI ethics and governance** — frameworks that are actually useful, not just compliance theater

## How to write a new post

Create a file in `_posts/` with the naming convention `YYYY-MM-DD-your-title.md` and add this front matter:

```yaml
---
layout: post
title: "Your Post Title"
date: 2026-04-11
description: "A one-sentence summary shown on the blog listing page."
tags: [tag1, tag2]
---
```

Everything below the front matter is standard Markdown.
